### Preparing for changes
To prevent cases of changes leading to incidences, it is good practice to ensure that changes are scheduled and well-coordinated. This can be done by using chatrooms to announce changes and find any possible areas of collisions.
### Peer reviews over board approvals
Instead of having a board approval before development, er can have peer reviews that is done during development with people who are well versed with what is being developed. This reduces the long time one might take to do an approval.
### What makes a good pull request?
When creating pull requess, it is advisable to enrich it with comments, documentation, send it for reviews either to peers or the code owner and attach any other relevant artifacts to ensure that the pull request is effective.
### Get rid of corporate red-tape
Get rid of processes that do not bring any value or improve quality of work being churned out.
