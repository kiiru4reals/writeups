### Make low-risk changes to standard changes
Re-categorize low risk changes to standard changes, without necessarily going through a [Change Advisory Board](https://www.atlassian.com/itsm/change-management/change-advisory-board) by building confidence among stakeholders on how low risk changes do not cause nightmares in production but also ensuring that the documentation of the changes is solid.
### Reduce TAT for CAB
Reduce time taken for normal changes to go through CAB by ensuring that the required documentation is in order and proposed changes are linked to the user story documentation. This could be through Jira or similar tools.
### Meeting compliance and audit in the middle
Leverage on documentation to bridge the gap between Developers, Operations, Audit and compliance to show that due-process is followed.
