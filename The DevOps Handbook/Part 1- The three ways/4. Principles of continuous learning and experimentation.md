Learning should always be encouraged by preventing cases of blaming and punishing people when things go wrong. When things go wrong a blameless postmoterm should be carried out as a learning point.
### Making improvement part of daily work
Daily improvement should be inculcated in people's daily work. This can be achieved by having days where you work on making performance improvements, bug fixes among other means.
### Democratization of knowledge
Find a way to make lessons learnt shareable to other people, so that knowledge transcends to different teams in the organization. You can explore having a wiki with mistakes that were made, how they were resolved. This can then be linked to the code using comments.
### Building resilience patterns
Implement resilience patterns in your work so as to easily detect problems and get feedback on places you can improve on. Read on [Chaos Monkey here](https://netflix.github.io/chaosmonkey/).
### Leaders empowering their teams
Leaders should be close to the work but also be in position to guide on improvement processes and how experimentation is done to ensure that customers get maximum value.
