### Perennial problems
Create time in your organization to solve problems that have been dogging your teams, get [everybody together and solve the problem.]([[3. The second way- The principles of feedback#Collectively attacking problems]]) Work on creating avenues for knowledge exchange between other teams. Organize, attend and/or sponsor DevOps events so as to create avenues for your teams to learn new things and share with other team players.
