To reduce tiem to market, [Big fish games](https://www.bigfishgames.com/) came up with an innovative way of having their operations team integrated with their development teams. This was done by splitting the trams into two so as to avoid increasing headcount.
### Building centralized tools
Use centralized tools to empower development teams. These tools can be shared by different teams so as to cover more products. Options available should be open to competition from external vendors so as to boost innovation.
### All hands on deck
Operations teams should be involved in daily standups and other dev rituals so as to enable them to understand whiat is being done and adequately prepare for release. Release managers and other production teams need to understand what the developers are building, why it is being built and also contribute in giving insights from an architectural perspective.
