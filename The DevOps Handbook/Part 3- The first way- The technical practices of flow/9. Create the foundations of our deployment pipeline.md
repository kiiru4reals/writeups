### Dev == UAT == Prod
Ensire that developers have environments that mirror production environments as early as possible. This gives them an idea of how the application will work in a production environment and reduces cases of "it works on my machine."
### Version Control Systems are your sources of truth
Ensure that everything (code and infra-related code) is checked into version control systems. It is recommended that you use Infrastructure as Code for immutability of your infrastructure. This also prevents people from manually making updates on the infrastructure. To effect this, you are recommended to have your infrastructure rebuild itself after a short period of time so as to make it unsustainable to do manual patch work.
### Containerization is king
Leverage on containerization to abstract infrastructure and make it possible to have an environment similar to production available to developers.
