### Building for productivity, testability and safety
It is advisable to design your product in a way that it is loosely coupled to facilitate productivity, testability and safety.
### No bad architecture
There is no bad architecture, they all cater to different needs such as time to market, scalability etc. Architectures should continuously evolve overtime to cater for different needs.
### Strangler Fig Application Pattern
The Strangler Fig Application Pattern is a software modernization pattern used to gradually replace a legacy system with a new system without shutting the old one down all at once.
Read more on Strangler Fig on [Netflix ](https://caffeinatedcoder.medium.com/netflixs-microservices-migration-from-monolith-to-700-services-8caa8e5bc574) and [Amazon](https://docs.aws.amazon.com/prescriptive-guidance/latest/cloud-design-patterns/strangler-fig.html).
